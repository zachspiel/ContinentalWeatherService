About Us

- Our Mission -

To provide a time-based forecast including alerts and warnings of hazardous weather through traditional analysis and the latest numerical models. Severe alerts and warnings are provided by phone, fax and internet.

- Our Products -

Clients will be provided with 2 primary forecast a day; 5am and 2pm CDT. They can be reassured as they can phone any time of day as weather conditions are constantly on the move. Climate data can also be provided to our clientele. Main forecast areas; NE Illinois, SO Wisconsin; Hudson Vally, NY. Other services provided are live and taped radio broadcasts on demand.
